# How to add SVG Icons ?

To add your favorite tech icons to enhance your profile, you need to follow four simple steps.

### 1. Visit the [simpleicons](https://simpleicons.org/) website.

![SimpleIconHomePage](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/62666287/231270135-a32cb732-46e3-429b-9d94-07dfb4a89100.png)

### 2. Search for the icon which you want to add to your profile.

![SimpleIconSearch](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/62666287/231272249-a56f0b4f-cb58-4575-a126-7857f633dbac.png)

### 3. Hover on the icon, and you will find a double-squared box. Click on it to copy the SVG.

![SimpleIconNextSelect](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/62666287/231270250-dfad3091-42c1-4469-9077-def68ff13c44.png)

### 4. Now paste it into the icon field.

![SimpleIconNextPaste](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/62666287/231270235-63064a32-784b-4318-a917-572f814f5593.png)

And you are ready to show off your skills to the world.
